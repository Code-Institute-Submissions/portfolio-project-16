MADE FROM THE FINEST INGREDIENTS AROUND

Home of Stochkolms's best burrito, Tommy's Burritos has been serving burrito addicts in Vasastan since 2014.

It is our mission to serve you the best tasting burritos in a friendly and fast environment where everyone feels at home.

Our mantra in life is nothing should be taken too seriously, except for rolling that burrito. We invite all of you to come down to share some nachos with guacamole, a beer, some good times and some great stories!

DAILY SPECIALS

Apart from our flagship burritos (shown the menu below), we offer new specials every day and constantly add new things to our menu. We want to keep our customers excited and always coming back for more!
